Clean-Up Drive
(April 2023):

The organization organizes a large-scale Clean-Up Drive, mobilizing volunteers to remove litter and pollutants from local parks, beaches, and waterways, fostering a sense of community stewardship and environmental responsibility.

Reforestation Project
(November 2023):

The organization initiates a Reforestation Project, partnering with local communities and government agencies to plant trees and restore degraded ecosystems, combating deforestation and mitigating climate change.
